Privacy Policy

At Vaidya Bandhu, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide when you fill out the membership form or contact us, including:

  • Your name
  • Address
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Optional details like PAN and Aadhaar

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and manage your membership
  • Deliver healthcare-related services and support
  • Send important updates and reminders
  • Share health tips, offers, and promotions (only if consented)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • When required to provide our services (e.g., to partner hospitals or doctors)
  • When legally required by government authorities

4. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies or third-party tools (like Google Analytics) to enhance user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or update your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
